Sentence Card Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-27

Summary

Sentence Card is a language-learning app. We collect the minimum information needed to let you sign in, sync purchase status, and keep your study progress available on your device and connected services.

Data We Collect

  • Account data: email address used for one-time-code sign in.
  • Purchase data: entitlement and transaction status returned by Apple and RevenueCat.
  • Study data: progress, mistakes, favorites, selected level, and reminder preferences.
  • Device permission data: notification permission status, microphone permission, and speech recognition results used during pronunciation practice.

How We Use Data

  • Sign you in with email OTP.
  • Restore and verify premium access.
  • Store study progress and preferences.
  • Power reminder notifications.
  • Support pronunciation and speech practice features.

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase for authentication and backend data services.
  • RevenueCat for in-app purchase entitlement management.
  • Apple services for App Store billing and platform permissions.

Data Storage

  • Some study data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.
  • Authentication and backend-linked account data may be stored in Supabase.
  • Purchase state may be processed by Apple and RevenueCat.

Account Deletion

If you create an account, you must be able to request full account deletion. The production app should provide an in-app deletion flow or a public webpage linked from the app and App Store listing.
