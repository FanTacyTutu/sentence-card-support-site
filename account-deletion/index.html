Sentence Card Account Deletion

Last updated: 2026-03-28

Current Status

  • Email OTP sign-in exists via Supabase.
  • Sign out exists in the app.
  • Full account deletion requires the backend deletion function to be deployed.

Required Production Behavior

  • Users can find account deletion from the account or settings area.
  • Users understand that deletion removes their account and associated personal data.
  • Users confirm deletion intentionally.
  • Deletion is completed through a real backend process.

Recommended Flow

  1. Open the account screen in the app.
  2. Tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm the deletion request.
  4. The backend securely deletes the signed-in user from Supabase.
